COVID-19 Guidelines

The BG retreat will be a hybrid event that will accomodate participants virtually and with limited in-person attendence.

  • For those of you that plan to attend in person, if you have not received one (J&J/Jannsen) or two doses (Pfizer-BioNTech, Moderna, AstraZeneca) of the COVID-19 immunization, we recommend that you join us virtually to protect yourself and the community.
  • If you have been fully vaccinated and plan to attend in person, we require that you wear a mask inside the building.
  • If you are attending in-person you must bring your own mask. None will be supplied.
  • For speakers that wish to present in person, we ask that you bring and use your own computer.
